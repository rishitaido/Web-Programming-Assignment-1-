Recipe Comparison Table

A simple comparison of three example recipes.
Item Description Difficulty
Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta Sauté shrimp in butter, garlic, and lemon juice, then toss with spaghetti and parsley. Light, fresh, and done in under 20 minutes. Great for a quick weeknight dinner. Easy
Chicken Tikka Masala Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices, grill or pan-sear, then simmer in a creamy tomato-based sauce with garam masala and chili. Serve with basmati rice or naan. Bold, rich, and takes about an hour but worth it. Medium
Beef Wellington Seared beef tenderloin wrapped in mushroom duxelles, prosciutto, and puff pastry, baked until golden. Crispy outside, tender and juicy inside. A showstopper dish that requires careful timing and prep. Advanced
